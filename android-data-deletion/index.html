Data Deletion Request

Application: SafeShare: AI Face Blur (com.pierudzkisoftware.safeshare)

Developer: Kamil Pierudzki Oprogramowanie

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

This page describes what data SafeShare processes and how you can request its deletion. For a complete description of our data practices, please also review our Privacy Policy.

1. What Data SafeShare Processes

SafeShare does not operate any servers and does not collect or store your personal information on our infrastructure. Specifically:

  • Photos and videos you process in SafeShare are handled entirely on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your device.
  • No user account is required or created. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts.
  • No precise location is collected.

The only data associated with your use of SafeShare is collected by Google AdMob (our advertising partner) and may include:

  • Advertising ID (AAID): A resettable identifier provided by Android.
  • IP address: Used by Google for fraud prevention and approximate location.
  • Ad interaction data: Impressions, clicks, and basic device information (operating system, device model).

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. See Google's advertising policies for details.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

Option A — In the SafeShare app (recommended)

  1. Open SafeShare on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon on the welcome screen).
  3. Tap Ads Privacy Settings to open the consent form and withdraw your consent for personalized ads.

Option B — In your device's system settings

To reset or delete your Advertising ID at the operating system level:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Reset advertising ID.
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable Allow Apps to Request to Track.

Option C — Directly with Google

To manage or delete your Google advertising profile (which is shared across all apps and websites that use Google ads, not just SafeShare):

  • Visit Google My Ad Center.
  • Sign in with your Google account and adjust or delete your ad personalization data.

Option D — By email (for written confirmation or GDPR Article 17 requests)

If you require a written confirmation, or you want to exercise your rights under GDPR Article 17 ("right to be forgotten"), you may contact us at:

  • kamil.pierudzki.software@gmail.com

Please include the application name (SafeShare) in your message. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

3. What Gets Deleted, What Is Retained

Data type What happens after your request Retention by Google
Photos and videos Never collected. Nothing to delete on our side. N/A
Advertising ID (AAID) Reset or deleted at the device level. Future ad requests are no longer linked to the previous identifier. Google retains historical ad logs in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
IP address Not stored after each ad request on our side. Aggregated and retained by Google per their policies.
Consent state (UMP) Reset when you withdraw consent in the app. Cleared completely when you uninstall SafeShare. N/A — stored locally on your device only.
Local cache (temporary processing files) Cleared automatically by Android when storage is needed, or manually via Settings → Apps → SafeShare → Clear cache. Fully removed on uninstall. N/A — local only.

4. Children's Data

SafeShare is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used our application in a way that would result in data collection, please contact us using the email above and we will take immediate steps to address the request.
